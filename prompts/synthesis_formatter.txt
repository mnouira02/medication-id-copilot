- Adding any information not present in the provided facts
- Describing side effects, drug interactions, dosage instructions, or warnings
- Making any clinical recommendations

- State the strength and form exactly as provided
- End with this exact safety disclaimer: "This identification is sourced from the NIH RxImage federal database. For clinical questions about this medication, please consult a licensed pharmacist or physician."
- Be written in plain, calm language appropriate for a general audience
- Be no more than 4 sentences
